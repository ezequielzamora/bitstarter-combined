What does it do?

The ThermoScope measures temperature in your food and instantly sends it to your phone.

How does it work?

It's very simple. Download and install the ThermoScope app. Wirelessly connect your ThermoScope. Measure and monitor on your phone.

Why is it better?

It's better because it works great and it's very easy to use. It gives accurate measurements to withing 0.5 degrees, It provides temperature information withing 5 seconds of measurement, rugged, easy to clean, and it will last for years.

Is the ThermoScope for me?

The ThermoScope is for everyone who could use a bit of help in the kitchen, it been invented by foodies and it's intended to be in the hands of foodies.

It works great for professional chefs, pastry chefs, tea enthusiasts, baristas, moms cooking alphabet soup or mac & cheese for their kids, students cooking ramen noodles in their college dorms, and super heroes trying to make the perfect wine reduction to save the world!.

What can I use my ThermoScope with?

Water, soups, sauces, dressings, reductions, liquid caramel, chocolate, ice cream, tea, coffee, dough, batter, cocktails and anything else you can get your hands on.

FAQ

Where does the name ThermoScope come from?
The name is a Spanish amalgamation of the Greek word thermos ("hot") and the word Telescope to represent the monitoring of temperature from afar.
What's a Floating Kitchen Thermometer?
It's a special kitchen utensil designed to measure liquid temperatures without the hassles of conventional thermometers.
